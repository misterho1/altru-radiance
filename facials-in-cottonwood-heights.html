Cottonwood Heights is one of the most distinct addresses in Salt Lake County. The neighborhood sits between the city and the mountains, with Big Cottonwood Canyon and Little Cottonwood Canyon both accessible from the eastern edge. The Cottonwood Paper Mill on the canyon road has been standing since 1883. The streets are quiet and residential, and the households here tend to be active, well-established, and deliberate about how they spend their time.

Altru Radiance sits 6 miles northwest. The drive down from Cottonwood Heights via I-215 or 6200 South takes about 12 minutes. Free building parking at 164 E 5900 S means no circling the block, and the by-appointment structure means the studio is ready when you arrive.

Skin and the Outdoor Life

Living near the canyons means regular exposure to altitude, dry air, wind, and reflected UV from snow and rock. These factors accumulate in the skin over time and show up as texture changes, uneven tone, and a general dulling of clarity. The cosmetic work at Altru Radiance addresses the visible surface. Microneedling with Skin Boosters and the Korean Aqua Peel are the two services Cottonwood Heights clients reach for first.

Microneedling at or below 1.5 mm depth is a cosmetic skin-resurfacing service. Combined with targeted skin boosters, it supports visible improvement in skin texture and surface quality over a series of sessions. The Korean Aqua Peel is a single-session water-based exfoliation and infusion treatment. Most clients see a visible difference in skin brightness and smoothness the same day.

Both are cosmetic services, not medical treatments. The distinction matters for clients in Cottonwood Heights who already see physicians and want to be clear about the lane they are working in.

Mechelle's Approach

Before focusing on any one area, Mechelle reads the whole picture. Skin quality, surface tension, how the face is presenting on the day of the session. This holistic assessment informs where she works and how. It is not a medical evaluation. The studio's scope stays entirely within cosmetic work throughout each session.

For Cottonwood Heights clients who are used to precise, well-organized services, this approach tends to feel coherent. There are no padding rituals or interchangeable steps. The session follows what the tissue communicates, which is why two clients with similar goals sometimes have noticeably different experiences in the chair.

Getting Here from Cottonwood Heights

The studio address is 164 E 5900 S, Suite A108, Murray, Utah 84107. From central Cottonwood Heights, take I-215 north to the 5900 South exit and continue east on 5900 South. Alternatively, take 6200 South westbound to State Street, then north to 5900 South. Total drive time is 10 to 14 minutes depending on your starting address. Free surface parking is available at the building. Suite A108 is on the first floor.

Note on scope: Every service at Altru Radiance is a cosmetic service, not a medical treatment. See the studio policies page for the full disclosure. If you have a skin concern requiring a physician, please see your doctor first.

A Good First Visit

For clients focused on skin texture and the visible effects of time outdoors, the Aqua Peel is the easiest starting point. One session, no series commitment, visible result the same day. From there, most clients want to understand microneedling or the Facial Architecture Ritual as a next step.

For clients who are curious about the structural side of the work, the Restorative Buccal Facial is the session that answers the question. It is unlike any other facial service in the area. The visible change at the jaw and surface tissue tends to reframe how clients think about facial work entirely.

Mechelle takes clients Tuesday through Saturday. Wednesday and Thursday evenings are available until 7:30 pm, which fits most work schedules. Cottonwood Heights clients often find the post-work midweek slot convenient, with the drive home via I-215 in the opposite direction of peak traffic.

Questions

Common Questions

How far is the studio from Cottonwood Heights?

The studio is at 164 E 5900 S, Suite A108 in Murray. From central Cottonwood Heights the drive is roughly 12 minutes via I-215 north. From the canyon mouth neighborhoods it is slightly longer, around 14 minutes. Free parking at the building.

Which service is right for clients who spend a lot of time outdoors?

The Korean Aqua Peel and Microneedling with Skin Boosters are both well suited to clients managing the visible effects of altitude, UV, and dry air on skin. The Aqua Peel is a single-session cosmetic option. Microneedling works better over a series of three to six sessions.

Is microneedling a medical procedure?

No. Altru Radiance offers microneedling at or below 1.5 mm depth, which is within the Utah Master Esthetician scope for cosmetic services. It is not a medical procedure, and no physician referral is required. See the studio policies page for more detail on scope.

What happens in the Soothe service?

The Soothe is a 75-minute full-body manual lymphatic cosmetic service with emphasis on the neck and torso. Mechelle works from the lower body upward. Most clients appear visibly less puffy after the session. It is a cosmetic service, not a medical treatment.

How do I book?

Book directly online at the Reserve link above. Sessions fill most quickly for Wednesdays and Thursday evenings. If you have questions about which service fits your goals, reach out via the contact page before booking.
