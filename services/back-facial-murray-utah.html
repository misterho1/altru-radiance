Back Facial:
Deep Cleanse
& Skin Clarity

The skin on your back is often the most neglected — harder to reach, rarely part of a daily skincare routine, and prone to the same congestion, buildup, and texture concerns as the face.

50 minDuration
$150Investment
Murray, UtahLocation

Book This Service

The Back Facial is a dedicated, professional treatment that gives that skin the same level of attention and care as any facial service. Using professional cleansing, gentle exfoliation, extractions where needed, and hydrating finishing treatment, this session leaves the back visibly clearer and the body genuinely at ease.

This is a deeply relaxing, therapeutic experience designed to clarify, smooth, and refresh skin that's been overlooked.

Key Benefits

  • Deep cleansing and pore clarification
  • Gentle exfoliation for smoother skin texture
  • Professional extractions for congested or blemish-prone areas
  • Hydration and skin-barrier support
  • Full-body relaxation — the back holds significant tension that responds beautifully to skilled touch

Who This Is For

  • Congestion, blemishes, or breakouts on the back
  • Rough or uneven skin texture
  • Pre-event prep — weddings, vacations, special occasions
  • Anyone who wants to extend their skincare routine beyond the face
  • Those wanting a deeply relaxing treatment that supports overall skin health

What to Expect

This is a comfortable, restorative session from start to finish. There is no clinical intensity here — just skilled, attentive work on skin that deserves care. You will leave feeling relaxed, refreshed, and noticeably clearer.

No specific preparation is required. Simply arrive comfortable and ready to rest.
