The Case for Consistency

Your Body Learns.
Every Session Builds.

Fascia has memory. The muscles of the face and jaw develop holding patterns over years — patterns reinforced by stress, screen time, sleep position, and emotional tension. A single session can release what's acute. Consistent work is what retrains the pattern entirely.

This is the same principle that underlies every lasting structural change: repetition creates new defaults. Monthly buccal fascia work builds on each previous session, progressively softening held patterns and helping your body establish a new baseline — one that is lighter, more symmetrical, and more aligned.

1
Session 1–3: Establishing the Foundation

Initial sessions focus on softening held tension, supporting lymphatic flow, and working with the surface fascia. You will notice results immediately — and progressively.

2
Ongoing: Retraining the System

With each monthly session, we work deeper into the tissue, reinforce the structural changes from previous sessions, and address new patterns as they emerge. Your face begins to hold itself differently between sessions.

3
Long-Term: A New Baseline

Over time, monthly maintenance becomes the foundation of how you age. Not corrective — regenerative. The goal is a face and body that continually reorganize toward ease, symmetry, and vitality rather than accumulating tension.

Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

How does the membership commitment work?
The first three months are non-cancellable and non-refundable — this is the structured commitment that makes consistent fascial work possible. After the initial term, the membership continues month to month, and you can end it by emailing mechelle@altruradiance.com with 30 days notice; cancellations are confirmed back in writing. The membership cannot be paused.
Can I gift my monthly session?
Yes. If you can't use a monthly session, you can gift it to a friend or family member. Notify me at least 24 hours in advance and ensure your guest has completed their intake form. Sessions do not roll over from month to month.
When am I billed?
Membership billing runs on the 5th of each month. If you join before the 5th, your first billing cycle begins immediately. If you join after the 5th, your first session is available right away at the membership rate — and your recurring billing begins on the following 5th. Either way, your session is confirmed once your first payment clears. You'll receive a receipt via email each billing cycle.
What if my payment fails?
If a payment doesn't go through on the 5th, I'll reach out by email. You'll have 5 days to update your payment method — appointments are simply paused until everything is sorted. Once payment clears, you're back on schedule.
Can I use my discount on any product?
The 10% discount applies to all retail products available through Altru Radiance and all add-on services booked during your monthly visit. It is applied automatically — no code needed.

A Note on Commitment
