Three short steps between now and your session. Lymph moves best in a hydrated body and a calm nervous system — small preparations make a visible difference.

II The Day Before

Hydrate well

Lymph is mostly water. The more hydrated you are leading in, the more we can move during your session. Aim for clear, frequent water intake the day before.

  • Drink water steadily through the day
  • Limit alcohol and excess salt
  • Avoid heavy meals within 2 hours of your appointment
  • Reduce caffeine the morning of
III Day Of

Arrive ready

Wear comfortable, loose clothing — drainage work involves the face and full neck. Plan to drink water after, and notice the difference over the next 24 hours.

  • Loose-fitting clothing, ideally a soft top
  • No heavy makeup on the day of
  • Plan to use the restroom soon after — drainage often triggers it
  • Continue hydrating for the rest of the day
