Choosing the Right Program

Where are you in the work?

Programs at Altru Radiance match stages of fascial progression, not a marketing funnel. Start with the program that fits your current state, not the one with the biggest discount.

If you're new here
Start with the Welcome Bundle. Two sessions across two disciplines give you and Mechelle a clear baseline: what your fascia is holding, where lymphatic stagnation is showing up, how your nervous system responds to manual work. The structural assessment that comes out of those two sessions becomes the plan for everything that follows.
If you're ready to commit to change
Choose the Foundation Package. Three sessions over six weeks is the minimum window in which fascia actually remodels. Cumulative work opens held patterns in sequence: the first session opens the most accessible layer, the second reaches into what was protected, the third stabilizes the new arrangement. Photo progress tracking documents the structural change.
If you've finished a series and want to hold the work
Move into the Maintenance Membership. Monthly buccal fascia work builds on every previous session, progressively softening held patterns and helping your body settle into a new structural baseline. The 3-month minimum is intentional: monthly is the cadence at which fascia retains memory of the new shape.
