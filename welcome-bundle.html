What is the Welcome Bundle at Altru Radiance?+

The Welcome Bundle is a two-session introductory package for new clients at Altru Radiance in Murray, Utah. Session one is the Restorative Buccal Facial — 110 minutes of intraoral hands-on fascia work with the buccinator, masseter, and pterygoid muscles, combined with lymphatic drainage and hands-on facial fascia work. Session two is SOOTHE, full-body lymphatic drainage that gives Mechelle access to the whole body. Together they provide a whole-picture assessment and a personalized plan. $400 total — $550 value.

What does the buccal facial actually do to the face?+

The buccal facial works intraorally — gloved hands inside the mouth work with the buccinator, masseter, and pterygoid muscles directly. These are the deep jaw and cheek muscles that carry the look and feel of the lower face. Working with the held tension in these areas softens the lower face, supports a sense of lift, and brings visible change — without needles or downtime. A cosmetic facial, not a medical treatment. Murray, Utah.

How is lymphatic drainage different from a regular massage?+

Lymphatic drainage uses light rhythmic manual techniques that support lymphatic flow — the body’s fluid drainage pathway. Unlike a conventional spa massage it does not compress muscle tissue. It moves lymphatic fluid through the vessels, supporting a lighter feel, reduced puffiness, and the appearance of body definition. The SOOTHE session at Altru Radiance works the full body, making it a whole-body experience rather than a localized one. Salt Lake Valley.

Is the Welcome Bundle right for first-time clients?+

Yes. The Welcome Bundle is specifically designed as an entry point for new clients. It introduces the fascia-informed facial work (the Restorative Buccal Facial) and the full-body lymphatic work (SOOTHE), giving Mechelle access to the whole picture before recommending a longer series. After both sessions you receive a personalized plan. Clients from Murray, Salt Lake City, Millcreek, Holladay, Sandy, and across Salt Lake Valley begin here.

How is Altru Radiance different from a med spa or day spa?+

Altru Radiance is neither a med spa nor a day spa. It is a fascia-informed aesthetic wellness studio offering hands-on esthetic techniques grounded in anatomy: the Restorative Buccal Facial, lymphatic drainage, the Facial Architecture Ritual, microneedling, Aqua Peel, and hands-on facial fascia work. No injectables. No downtime. Mechelle is a Licensed Master Esthetician in Murray, Utah whose hands-on work looks at the whole picture of how faces and bodies hold tension — not just the surface.
