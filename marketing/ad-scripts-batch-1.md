## Production notes (apply to every script)

1. **Continuity:** one anchor image per script; every scene of that script seeds from it. Same woman, same outfit within a same-day sequence. Fresh generations per camera position, never duplicated frames.
2. **De-AI recipe:** every visual prompt should carry: "shot on iPhone, natural light, slight film grain, visible pores and real skin texture, imperfect framing, no beauty filter."
3. **Imagery scope:** esthetician facial and neck/collarbone work only. Client always face-up. Nothing that reads as massage-table back work.
4. **VO delivery:** conversational, unhurried, no announcer energy. Every line was written to be read aloud — if a line trips the tongue in recording, cut words, don't add them.
5. **Compliance recap per script:** no TMJ/pain/medical claims (scripts 4, 7, 10 walk closest to the line — they stay on appearance, feel, and experience). No permanence claims anywhere; scripts 7 and 8 state temporariness/series truth on purpose — the research shows honesty converts. Only verified facts used: $250/90min buccal, Murray location, Master Esthetician education-first reviews (quoted verbatim).
6. **Quote sourcing:** every quoted overlay traces to CUSTOMER-RESEARCH.md or its Reddit quote banks (same folder). Reddit quotes are presented anonymously ("someone online"), Google reviews with first name + "Google review."
