Before You Arrive

What to Expect

Altru Radiance is a private, single-room studio. There's no waiting room energy, no product-pushing, no pressure. Just focused, intentional work and a space where your nervous system can genuinely settle.

First visits include a full intake and structural consultation before we begin. Plan for about 15 extra minutes.

Comfortable, relaxed clothing is ideal. For facial treatments, you'll be lying down with your head and neck accessible. A loose collar or button-down makes transitions easy. Avoid wearing heavy makeup if possible; we'll remove it at the start of your session.

Intraoral work is deeply intentional but not painful. Many clients describe a pressure-and-release sensation, particularly in areas that hold tension (jaw, cheeks, temples). Most clients find it profoundly relaxing once they settle in. Nitrile gloves are used throughout and fit, pressure, and pacing are always adjusted to your comfort.

Many clients see visible changes after a single session, particularly with the Restorative Buccal Facial, manual lymphatic drainage, and the Facial Architecture Ritual. For lasting structural change, a series of 3–6 sessions spaced 2–4 weeks apart is recommended. Results compound over time as fascia releases and the body learns to hold the new pattern.

Please let me know before booking if you have: active skin infections, open wounds, recent facial surgery (within 6 months), injectables or volume injection injections in the last 4 weeks, a history of jaw surgery, active dental work, or any autoimmune conditions affecting connective tissue. Microneedling has additional contraindications. A full intake form is completed at your first visit to ensure every treatment is appropriate for you.

Yes. Many treatments pair beautifully. The Restorative Buccal Facial is commonly combined with lymphatic drainage and red light therapy for a comprehensive structural session. Microneedling is generally scheduled as a standalone appointment to allow for proper recovery assessment. I'll make a recommendation based on your goals and skin's current state during your consultation.
